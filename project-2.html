Project Overview

The Face Emotion Detection Project is a machine learning and deep learning-based system designed to accurately detect the emotions of individuals. This project leverages the power of efficientnet and vit transformer models as its primary models. However, to further enhance the accuracy and performance of the system, a meta model based on a stacked ensemble approach is proposed. EfficientNet is a state-of-the-art convolutional neural network (CNN) architecture known for its remarkable performance in image classification tasks. It efficiently balances model depth, width, and resolution to achieve high accuracy while minimizing computational complexity. ViT, or Vision Transformer, is a transformer-based architecture originally designed for natural language processing tasks but has also shown exceptional capabilities in computer vision tasks. It leverages self-attention mechanisms to capture global image relationships and has achieved impressive results in image classification. To further enhance the system's accuracy and robustness, a meta model based on a stacked ensemble approach is proposed. The stacked ensemble combines the predictions of EfficientNet, ViT, and an additional simple CNN model. By aggregating the outputs of these models, the system can leverage their diverse strengths and capture a more comprehensive understanding of facial expressions and emotions.

The meta model combines the predictions of efficientnet, vit transformer, and a simple CNN (Convolutional Neural Network) model. By leveraging the strengths of these diverse models, the system aims to achieve improved accuracy in emotion detection. The stacked ensemble technique allows the models to complement each other, taking advantage of their individual capabilities and combining their predictions to make more informed decisions. This enhanced accuracy can serve as a valuable tool in various applications, particularly in online video conferences, where having a better understanding of participants' emotions is crucial for effective communication and engagement. By accurately summarizing the emotions of individuals, the system can provide valuable insights and assist in creating a more empathetic and inclusive environment.
